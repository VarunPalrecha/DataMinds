Frequently Asked Questions

The main goals of descriptive analysis are to provide a clear understanding of the dataset, identify patterns and trends, summarize and visualize the data, and derive meaningful insights. It helps in exploring the characteristics of the data, identifying outliers, and gaining an overall picture of the dataset's distribution.

The main goals of predictive analysis are to make accurate predictions about future outcomes, identify factors that significantly influence those outcomes, and understand the relationships and patterns in the data that contribute to those predictions. It helps in making informed decisions, optimizing processes, and planning for the future based on data-driven insights.

Some commonly used techniques in descriptive analysis include measures of central tendency (mean, median, mode), measures of dispersion (standard deviation, range), frequency distributions, histograms, bar charts, scatter plots, and summary statistics. These techniques help in summarizing and visualizing the data, gaining insights into its characteristics, and understanding the overall distribution.

Some commonly used techniques in predictive analysis include regression analysis, decision trees, random forests, support vector machines, neural networks, and time series analysis. These techniques help in building predictive models by identifying relationships between variables, capturing patterns and trends in the data, and making predictions based on historical or training data.

Descriptive analysis focuses on summarizing and describing the main features of a dataset, providing insights into its past or present characteristics. It helps in understanding the data, identifying patterns, and gaining overall knowledge about the dataset. On the other hand, predictive analysis aims to make predictions or forecasts about future outcomes based on historical data. It involves building models and algorithms to identify patterns and relationships in the data that can be used to make informed predictions about future events or behaviors.
